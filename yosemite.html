Yosemite National Park

Witness the awe-inspiring beauty of Yosemite National Park, where towering granite cliffs, cascading waterfalls, and ancient trees create an unforgettable experience. Explore miles of scenic trails, from the iconic Half Dome to the serene Yosemite Valley. Yosemite offers a breathtaking journey into nature's grandeur.

Waterfalls

Yosemite's waterfalls are some of the tallest and most beautiful in the world, with Yosemite Falls standing at 2,425 feet. From the powerful roar of Bridalveil Fall to the misty beauty of Vernal Fall, each one offers a unique experience. You can hike to the base or admire them from afar!

Wildlife

Yosemite is home to incredible wildlife, from playful deer and curious squirrels to the iconic black bear. Keep an eye out for coyotes, bobcats, and a variety of birds soaring above the cliffs. While spotting a bear can be exciting, remember to keep your distance and store food safely to protect both you and the animals.

Half Dome

Half Dome is Yosemite's most iconic landmark, standing nearly 5,000 feet above the valley floor with its distinct granite face. The hike to the summit is a challenging adventure, featuring steep switchbacks and a cable climb at the top. Its striking presence is a well-known symbol of Yosemite's rugged beauty.
